When you come back tomorrow, just remember the two steps to wake your bot up:

(1) Open a terminal in the folder and start the API: 
source venv/bin/activate && uvicorn main:app --reload
(2) Open a second terminal and get your URL: 
 $ source venv/bin/activate 
 $ python get_temp_WebhookURL.py 
(Copy that URL into your LINE Developer Console, and you are back online!)
